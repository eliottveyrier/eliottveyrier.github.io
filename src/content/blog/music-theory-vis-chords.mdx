## Instrument Span

```jsx
<InstrumentSpan category="strings">violin</InstrumentSpan>
<InstrumentSpan cat="strings">violin</InstrumentSpan>
```

<InstrumentSpan cat="woodwinds">Woodwinds</InstrumentSpan> like
<InstrumentSpan cat="woodwinds">flute</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan cat="woodwinds">oboe</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan cat="woodwinds">clarinet</InstrumentSpan>, and
<InstrumentSpan cat="woodwinds">bassoon</InstrumentSpan> are represented in brown.

<InstrumentSpan category="brass">Brass</InstrumentSpan> like
<InstrumentSpan category="brass">trumpet</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan category="brass">horn</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan category="brass">trombone</InstrumentSpan>, and
<InstrumentSpan category="brass">tuba</InstrumentSpan> are represented in gold.

<InstrumentSpan category="percussion">Percussion</InstrumentSpan> like
<InstrumentSpan category="percussion">timpani</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan category="percussion">snare drum</InstrumentSpan>, and
<InstrumentSpan category="percussion">cymbals</InstrumentSpan> are represented in blue.

<InstrumentSpan category="keyboards">Keyboards</InstrumentSpan> like
<InstrumentSpan category="keyboards">piano</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan category="keyboards">organ</InstrumentSpan>, and
<InstrumentSpan category="keyboards">harpsichord</InstrumentSpan> are represented in green.

<InstrumentSpan category="voices">Voices</InstrumentSpan> like
<InstrumentSpan category="voices">soprano</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan category="voices">alto</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan category="voices">tenor</InstrumentSpan>, and
<InstrumentSpan category="voices">bass</InstrumentSpan> are represented in a light pink.

<InstrumentSpan category="synths">Synths</InstrumentSpan> are represented in purple.

<InstrumentSpan category="strings">Strings</InstrumentSpan> like
<InstrumentSpan category="strings">violin</InstrumentSpan>,
<InstrumentSpan category="strings">harp</InstrumentSpan>, and
<InstrumentSpan category="strings">cello</InstrumentSpan> are represented in red.

this can be applied to notes :

```jsx
<NoteSpan
    note="C5"
    colorBy="instrument"
    instrumentCategory="woodwinds"
/>
```
