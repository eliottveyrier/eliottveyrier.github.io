### Range and tuning

The practical range of the violin in an orchestra ranges from <NoteSpan note="G3" /> up to <NoteSpan note="E7"/>. 
In the classical era, the upper limit was considered to be <NoteSpan note="A7"/> [^1]

On a given string, two octaves is a good limit, with an octave and a fifth being a more comfortable range.

[^1]: Samuel Adler, *The study of Orchestration*
